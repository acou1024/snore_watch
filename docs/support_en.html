Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What permissions does the app need?

A: The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone (Required): Used to monitor ambient sound and record snoring
  • Notifications (Optional): Used to send reminders when snoring is detected

Q: Why isn't the app detecting snoring?

A: Please check the following:

  • Make sure microphone permission is granted
  • Adjust the decibel threshold setting (recommended: 55-65dB)
  • Place your phone 1-2 meters from your head
  • Avoid placing the phone under a pillow or in an obstructed location

Q: What's the difference between the two monitoring modes?

A:

  • Record Only (Mode A): Only records snoring sounds, no alarm music will play
  • Record & Alarm (Mode B): Plays music to remind you to adjust your sleeping position when continuous snoring is detected

Q: Where are recordings saved?

A: Recordings are saved in the app's local documents directory and are never uploaded to any server. You can play and delete recordings within the app.

Q: Does the app consume a lot of battery?

A: The app continuously monitors sound while running in the background, which does consume some battery. We recommend connecting your phone to a charger while using the app.

Q: How do I delete all data?

A: You can:

  • Delete recording files one by one within the app
  • Uninstall the app to delete all local data

Usage Tips

  • Place your phone on a nightstand, about 1-2 meters from your head
  • Make sure the phone's microphone is not obstructed
  • Try "Record Only" mode first when using for the first time
  • Adjust the decibel threshold based on your actual situation
  • We recommend using the app while charging
