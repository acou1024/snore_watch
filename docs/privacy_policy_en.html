Privacy Policy

Snore Watch

Last Updated: January 2024

Important Notice: Snore Watch is a completely offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data to external servers. All your data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information Collection

This application uses the following device features during operation:

  • Microphone Permission: Used to monitor ambient sound decibel levels and detect snoring. Recording data is only saved locally on your device.
  • Notification Permission (Optional): Used to send reminder notifications when snoring is detected.
  • Screen Wake: Used to keep the screen on during alarms.

2. Data Storage

All data generated by this application is stored locally on your device:

  • Snoring recordings are stored in the app's local documents directory
  • User settings (such as guard duration, music selection, etc.) are stored locally
  • No data is uploaded to any server

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties because:

  • The app does not connect to any external servers
  • The app does not contain any advertising SDKs
  • The app does not contain any analytics tools
  • The app does not collect any user behavior data

4. Data Security

Your data security is protected by your device's system. We recommend:

  • Regularly clean up unnecessary recording files
  • Use your device's password or biometric features to protect your device
  • Regularly update your device's operating system

5. User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete recording files at any time within the app
  • You can revoke the app's microphone permission at any time
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@snorewatch.app
