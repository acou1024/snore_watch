Privacy Policy

Snore Watch

Last Updated: February 2026

Our Commitment: We respect and protect your privacy. This app does not collect, upload, or share any user data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Data Collection

Snore Watch only uses the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Used to monitor ambient sound levels and detect snoring patterns. Audio data is processed locally in real-time and is never recorded or uploaded.
  • Storage: Used to save snoring recordings to your device's local storage.
  • Notifications: Used to send reminder notifications when snoring is detected.
  • HealthKit (Optional, iOS): Used to sync sleep monitoring data to the iOS Health app. Data is only written with your authorization and we do not read any of your other health data.

2. Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for:

  • Real-time analysis of ambient sound to detect snoring
  • Saving snoring recordings locally for your playback
  • Triggering local alarm reminders
  • Generating sleep statistics reports (scores, history, trends)
  • Exporting CSV data and report images (shared only at your discretion)
  • Syncing sleep data to iOS Health app (requires your authorization)

3. Data Storage

100% Local Storage: All data (including recordings and user settings) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server or cloud service.

4. Data Sharing

We commit to:

  • Never sharing your data with third parties
  • Never selling your data
  • Never using data for advertising purposes
  • No ads or trackers in the app

5. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View: You can view all saved recordings within the app
  • Delete: You can delete any recording at any time
  • Export: Recordings are stored locally and freely accessible
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will delete all related data

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted on this page. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: support@snorewatch.app
